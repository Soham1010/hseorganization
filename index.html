Our Purpose

HSE the name of our organization states that "HUM SAB EK" which means that WE ARE ALL ONE and being one we all can serve our mother earth and can preserve resources for the future generation. The main purpose of HSE is to create awareness among people for social welfare and serve the society 

Planting Trees for a Greener Future

By donating to our tree-planting initiatives, you are helping create a greener environment and combat climate change. Your contribution will support the planting and maintenance of trees in our community, providing a host of environmental benefits such as cleaner air and water, increased biodiversity, and reduced carbon emissions. Join us in creating a more sustainable future for all.

About Our Organization

Our NGO, HSE Organization, is a student-led initiative dedicated to creating a better world through tree-planting, social welfare, and animal welfare initiatives. Our team is passionate about making a positive impact in our community, and we are committed to inspiring others to join us on this journey.

To stay updated on our activities and initiatives, follow us on Instagram (@hseorg) and Facebook (@hseorganisation). We believe in the power of collective action, and we welcome everyone to join us in our mission towards a more sustainable and compassionate world
